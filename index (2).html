    ABOUT EPIC 2018



    Advanced industrial technology in
    engineering physics

    The aim of this conference is to bring together students, scientists, and engineers who are involved in research and development in advanced industrial technology

  • Plant Wide Control
  • Advanced Process Control
  • Industrial Instrumentation
  • Sensor and Actuator
  • Energy System and Management
  • Thermal Science and Engineering
  • Physics in the Building Environment
  • Computational Material Designs
  • Industrial Health, Safety, and Environment
  • Medical Instrumentation
  • Illumination Design and Technology
  • Environmental Noise
  • Architectural Acoustics
  • Vibration Measurement and Control
  • Other Relevant Topics in Engineering Physics
  • Optimization, Modeling and Visualization Techniques
  • Renewable and Clean Energy Technologies
  • Advanced Material Science and Nanotechnology
  • Smart Computational and Communication Technology
  • Optics and Photonics
