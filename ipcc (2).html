ABOUT IPCC 2018



International Process Control Competition

Multivariable systems are generally and encountered in real industrial processes due to the complexity such as petrochemical industry, oil and gas industry, refinery industry, cement industry, power plants, etc. In order to achieve the well quantity and quality of production, it is necessary to perform a multivariable PID controller tuning.


THEME

“PID Controller Tuning for Multivariable System”


Event Description

  • There will be two stages of the competition, preliminary round and final round.
  • On preliminary round, each team should make an essay to propose a method of tuning PID for a multivariable system.
  • The selected teams will be continued to final round and will be provided the case study. The case study solution must be submitted as paper and presentation slides.
  • The winners will be presented at Engineering Physics International Conference.
