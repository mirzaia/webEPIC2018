The Department of Engineering Physics Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember proudly host:
International Process Control Competition 2018


Event Description
IPCC

  • The theme of this competition is "PID Controller Tuning for Multivariable System"
  • There will be two stages of the competition, preliminary round and final round.
  • On preliminary round, each team should be proposed the method to solve the problem related to the competition theme in the form of an essay.
  • The selected teams will be continued to final round and will be provided the case study. The case study solution must be submitted as camera ready paper and presentation slides.
