Registration


The structure of the full paper must be:

1.

Each team should submit essay in PDF format and registration form. Both files available at bit.ly/essaytemplateIPCC2018 and bit.ly/registrationformIPCC2018

2.

Send both files in compressed file (.rar) before July 15th, 2018 23.59 (GMT+7) to:
ipccompetition2018@gmail.com

3.

Put the subject: "Team Name_University_IPCC 2018" in the e-mail correspondence.

4.

Notification of acceptance will be announced at epic2018.its.ac.id/IPCC on August 27th, 2018

5.

The selected teams for the best essays will be announced at epic2018.its.ac.id/IPCC on September 3rd, 2018

6.

The selected teams should pay the competition fee by bank transfer to committee's bank account before September 7th, 2018
