general rules and regulation

GENERAL RULES AND REGULATION :

      1. Open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from all over the world.
      2. Each team consists of three persons, without substitution.
      3. The team is not allowed if all members are postgraduate student.
      4. Every member of the team must be from the same university, although they are allowed to be from differently departments.
      5. Oral presentation and paper should be written in English.
      6. The judges decision is final and no correspondence will be entered into by the judges in relation to that decision.
